\begin{ChineseAbstract}
面向不确定环境中的机器人自主决策问题，安全强化学习在提升策略性能的同时，仍面临约束违规风险与仿真到真实迁移困难等挑战。同时，小型多机器人在面对不确定环境中具有部署灵活、适应性强等特点，然而目前多智能体系统仍面临协同困难、任务目标与安全约束相互耦合等问题。围绕安全性保障与多机器人协同两个关键问题，本文从安全控制、多机器人编队策略以及系统级验证平台三个层面展开研究，提出了一套完整的安全强化学习方法与工程化验证体系。

（1）针对不确定环境中机器人自主决策过程中的安全问题，本文提出一种由流形安全滤波器、离线可达性分析及数据驱动扩展卡尔曼滤波器构成的统一框架。流形安全滤波器通过零空间投影将策略动作映射至约束流形切空间，并结合奖励校准机制，引导策略学习内在安全行为；可达性分析基于离线轨迹数据预计算环境相关安全区域，在部署阶段无需实时感知即可自适应调整安全裕度；数据驱动的扩展卡尔曼滤波器利用卷积神经网络学习噪声参数，实现测量协方差对速度相关传感器噪声的自适应建模。
实验结果表明，在 Goal 任务消融实验中，完整系统将平均安全代价由 PPO 基线的 64.59 降至 12.51，降幅达到 80.6\%；在 Webots E-puck 零样本部署实验中，碰撞率由无过滤条件下的 40.0\% 降至 0.0\%，位置误差由 0.051 m 降至 0.037 m，降幅为 27.5\%。上述结果说明，所提框架能够在降低约束违背风险的同时保持较好的迁移性与鲁棒性。
% 实验结果表明，该方法在多个安全强化学习基准任务中几乎实现零约束违背，显著优于对比方法。此外，在移动机器人仿真平台上的零样本部署实验进一步验证了所提框架在实际系统中的良好迁移性与鲁棒性。

（2）针对多机器人编队导航中任务目标相互耦合、安全约束同时存在的情况，本文提出一种基于几何约束的策略学习方法。该方法在 MAPPO 的动作决策基础上，引入几何约束过滤机制，对编队结构保持、机器人间安全距离以及避障要求进行统一处理，使策略输出不再只依赖奖励函数对行为进行间接约束，而是在动作执行前完成安全修正。
实验结果显示，在线形编队任务中，GCPL 相较于 MAPPO 将安全代价由 105.78 降至 18.53，降幅为 82.5\%；编队完成度由 10.00\% 提升至 71.00\%，成功率由 2.00\% 提升至 66.00\%。在圆形编队等复杂拓扑任务中，GCPL 相较于 MAPPO 将安全代价由 145.22 降至 34.40，降幅为 76.3\%，成功率由 0.50\% 提升至 28.00\%。上述结果表明，该方法能够在保证安全性的同时提升任务完成效率与编队稳定性。
% 实验结果显示，该方法能够在降低碰撞风险的同时保持较好的任务执行效率，编队形态也更加稳定，说明几何约束与强化学习策略的结合能够改善编队导航中安全性与性能之间的协调关系。

（3）为了检验上述方法在不同场景中的可用性，并降低强化学习算法训练环境与部署环境不一致带来的影响，本文设计并实现了一套面向多机器人安全强化学习的容器化仿真验证平台。平台采用分层架构组织仿真环境、算法接口、评估模块和结果展示模块，通过容器化方式统一运行依赖，减少不同实验配置之间的环境差异；同时，平台设置了标准化评估流程，用于对不同算法在相同任务条件下进行比较。在此基础上，本文将安全约束机制与多机器人协同策略接入平台，并通过实验分析其在安全约束满足、任务完成效果和编队一致性等方面的表现，从系统层面验证了所提方法的综合效果。

\end{ChineseAbstract}

\begin{EnglishAbstract}
    Aiming at the problem of robot autonomous decision-making in uncertain environments, safety reinforcement learning still faces challenges such as the risk of constraint violation and the difficulty of migration from simulation to reality while improving the performance of the strategy. At the same time, small multi-robots have the characteristics of flexible deployment and strong adaptability in the face of uncertain environments. However, multi-agent systems still face problems such as difficulty in coordination, coupling of mission objectives and security constraints. Focusing on the two key issues of security assurance and multi-robot collaboration, this paper studies from three aspects : security control, multi-robot formation strategy and system-level verification platform, and proposes a complete set of security reinforcement learning methods and engineering verification system.
    
    (1) Aiming at the security problem in the autonomous decision-making process of robots in uncertain environments, this paper proposes a unified framework consisting of manifold security filters, off-line reachability analysis, and data-driven extended Kalman filters. The manifold security filter maps the policy action to the constrained manifold tangent space through null space projection, and combines the reward calibration mechanism to guide the policy to learn the internal security behavior. Reachability analysis is based on the off-line trajectory data to pre-compute the environment-related security areas, and the security margin can be adaptively adjusted without real-time perception during the deployment phase. The  data-driven extended Kalman filter uses the convolutional neural network to learn the noise parameters, and realizes the adaptive modeling of the measurement covariance to the speed-related sensor noise. The experimental results show that this method almost achieves zero constraint violation in multiple security reinforcement learning benchmark tasks, which is significantly better than the comparison method. In addition, the zero-shot deployment experiment on the mobile robot simulation platform further verifies the good mobility and robustness of the proposed framework in the actual system.

    (2) A strategy learning method based on geometric constraints is proposed for multi-robot formation navigation in the case of mutual coupling of task objectives and simultaneous existence of security constraints. On the basis of MAPPO 's action decision-making, this method introduces a geometric constraint filtering mechanism to uniformly process the formation structure maintenance, the safe distance between robots and the obstacle avoidance requirements, so that the strategy output no longer only relies on the reward function to indirectly constrain the behavior, but completes the safety correction before the action is executed. The experimental results show that the method can reduce the collision risk while maintaining better task execution efficiency, and the formation shape is more stable, indicating that the combination of geometric constraints and reinforcement learning strategies can improve the coordination between security and performance in formation navigation.
    
    (3) In order to test the availability of the above methods in different scenarios and reduce the impact of the inconsistency between the training environment and the deployment environment of the reinforcement learning algorithm, this paper designs and implements a containerized simulation verification platform for multi-robot security reinforcement learning. The platform adopts a hierarchical architecture to organize the simulation environment, algorithm interface, evaluation module and result display module. The containerized method is used to unify the operation dependencies and reduce the environmental differences between different experimental configurations. At the same time, the platform sets up a standardized evaluation process to compare different algorithms under the same task conditions. On this basis, this paper integrates the security constraint mechanism and multi-robot cooperative strategy into the platform, and analyzes its performance in terms of security constraint satisfaction, task completion effect and formation consistency through experiments, and verifies the comprehensive effect of the proposed method from the system level.

\EnglishKeywords{Constraint Manifold; Multi-agent; SafeReinforcement Learning}
\end{EnglishAbstract}
